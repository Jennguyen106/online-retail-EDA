# Online Retail II — EDA & Customer Segmentation

## 📌 Overview
Exploratory Data Analysis and RFM Customer Segmentation 
on the Online Retail II dataset (UK retailer, 2009–2011).

**Dataset:** [Kaggle — Online Retail II UCI](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mashlyn/online-retail-ii-uci)  
**Tools:** Python · Pandas · Matplotlib · Seaborn · Scipy  
**Timeline:** June 30 – July 20, 2026

---

## 🎯 Business Questions
1. Which countries generate the highest revenue?
2. Are there seasonal patterns in sales?
3. How can we segment customers based on behavior?
4. Which customer segments are at risk of churning?
5. What are the top-selling products?
